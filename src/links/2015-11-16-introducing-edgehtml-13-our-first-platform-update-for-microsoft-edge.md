Tons of great stuff in this release. Notably: [the `picture` element](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/platform/status/pictureelement/), [the `srcset` (_with_ `w` descriptors) & `sizes` attributes](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/platform/status/extendedsrcsetandsizessupport/), CSS [`initial`](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/platform/status/cssinitialvalue/) and [`unset`](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/platform/status/cssunsetvalue/) keywords, [`a[download]`](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/platform/status/adownloadattribute/), [`:read-write` & `:read-only`](https://dev.modern.ie/platform/status/cssmutabilitypseudoclasses/), [`:in-range` & `:out-of-range`](https://dev.modern.ie/platform/status/cssrangepseudoclasses/), more ES2015 goodies, and a ton more. Hooray for evergreen!
