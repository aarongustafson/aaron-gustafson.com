<p>That beautiful bastion of standards-based e-commerce, <a href="http://www.karova.com">Karova</a>, has gotten a face lift. <a href="http://www.stuffandnonsense.co.uk/">Mr. Malarkey</a> deserves many kudos for yet another rich, engaging and playful design. And if you think <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20070608170510/www.karova.com/prices.aspx">the sales materials look good</a>, you should see the store management dashboard. I was offered a sneak peek and couldn’t help but fawn over its sophisticated simplicity. It’s not only usable, but it makes managing a web shop (dare I say it) kinda fun. For a little background on the redesign, read <a href="http://www.stuffandnonsense.co.uk/archives/designing_for_karovacom.html">Andy’s writeup</a>.</p>
<p>Now if only we could find a suitable partner to bring their product to the <span class="caps">US</span> (hint, hint).</p>
