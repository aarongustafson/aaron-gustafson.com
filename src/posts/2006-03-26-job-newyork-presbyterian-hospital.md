They will also be implementing a new <abbr title="Content Management System"><span class="caps">CMS</span></abbr> to run the show.So if you’re an experienced designer/developer with good information architecture skills (an ar comfortable working in a Microsoft shop) who is interested in leading a team (not just coding alone in the dark), give them a shout.</p>
