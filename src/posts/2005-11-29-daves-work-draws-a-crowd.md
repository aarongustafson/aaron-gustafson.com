<p>I just saw a copy of the latest issue of <cite>DMNews</cite> and <a href="https://blog.easy-designs.net/archives/getting-surreal-with-actionscript/">Dave’s hard work</a> garnered the <a href="http://www.wadsworthatheneum.org">Wadsworth Atheneum</a> <a href="http://www.dmnews.com/cgi-bin/artprevbot.cgi?article_id=34787&amp;dest=article">a feature story</a> and <a href="http://www.cronin-co.com">Cronin and Company</a> some major kudos. Here’s an excerpt from the article:</p>
<blockquote cite="http://www.dmnews.com/cgi-bin/artprevbot.cgi?article_id=34787&amp;dest=article">
<p>An online campaign initiated by the 161-year-old museum and developed by Glastonbury, CT, ad agency Cronin and Company Inc. doubled visits to the site at www.wadsworthatheneum.org. Components included a SurrealPainter Web tool, banner ads and the seeding of blogs.</p>

<!-- more -->

<p>Central to the campaign is the tool at www.wadsworthatheneum.org/painter. Visitors through Dec. 18 can choose from various colorful backgrounds and objects, then flip, copy, layer or scale them. Once completed, the online artwork can be titled, printed, published and e-mailed to family and friends.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Be sure to <a href="http://www.wadsworthatheneum.org/painter">make your own surrealist painting</a>, while you still can.</p>
