<p>My friends at <a href="http://www.gartner.com">Gartner</a> are hiring a new member for their web team. If you are within commuting distance of Stamford, CT (about 45 minutes outside of NYC by train), I highly recommend considering this job as I know first-hand how awesome this team is to work with.</p>

<!-- more -->

<p>You’ll need the following areas of knowledge (in addition to a thirst for more):</p>
<ul>
<li>The big 3: XHTML, JavaScript and CSS including browser compatibility issues and working with complex CSS (some of which is courtesy of yours truly)</li>
<li>XML & XSLT (specifically in converting XML to XHTML)</li>
<li>Some PHP/MySQL</li>
<li>RSS</li>
<li>web graphics (at least enough to translate from comp to code)</li>
<li>Teamsite templating (not necessary, but would be an advantage)</li>
</ul>
<p>If you’re interested, email my friend Aidan (aidan [dot] brewer [at] gartner [dot] com) and let him know I sent you.</p>
